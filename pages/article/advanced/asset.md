---
title: Asset
---

[![](asset/MDX.png)][1]

Put assets of a Markdown file into a folder with same base name in same path, as what [Hexo][2] or other **Static Web-site Generators** do.

For example:

```text
pages
  |-- article
        |-- asset.md
        |-- asset
              |-- MDX.png
```

[1]: https://mdxjs.com/
[2]: https://hexo.io
